Test examples for separator pattern detection:

Should be detected as separators (IsSeparatorPattern = true):
- "----------------------------- CY25 -----------------------------"
- "%%%%%%%% Q1 FY25 %%%%%%%%"
- "---- Sprint Planning ----"
- "%%%%%%% 2025 %%%%%%%"
- "--------%%%%--------"
- "--------------------- CY25H2 End ---------------------"

Should NOT be detected as separators (IsSeparatorPattern = false):
- "Feature - User Authentication"
- "Release Train Alpha"
- "Q1-2025 Planning Initiative"
- "CY25 Budget Approval Process"
- "Sprint-1 Development Tasks"

The algorithm checks if more than 60% of the title consists of dashes (-) or percent signs (%).
